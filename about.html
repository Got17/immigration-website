About Us

Trusted partner for immigration & business services in Hungary.

Our Mission

To simplify Hungarian administration for newcomers and foreign‑owned companies. We focus on transparency, security, and clear guidance so you can focus on living and working in Hungary.

What we do

  • Residence & work permit advisory, including renewals
  • Student pathways and arrival checklists
  • Company registration (Kft.) coordination with partner attorney
  • Document checklists and templates to reduce back‑and‑forth
  • Secure client portal for file sharing (GDPR‑ready)

How we work

  1. Consultation: Free 15‑min call to map your case and eligibility.
  2. Checklist: You receive a clear list of required documents.
  3. Preparation: We review your materials and prepare forms.
  4. Submission: We guide you through booking and submission steps.
  5. Follow‑up: Regular updates until completion.

We don’t guarantee outcomes—no one can—but we maximize your chance of success with orderly, complete applications.

Privacy‑First

GDPR‑aligned practices, EU hosting, minimal data collection, and client control over files.

Clarity

We prefer checklists, plain language, and proactive communication to avoid surprises.

Reliability

We track deadlines and statuses so your case keeps moving—without you having to chase.
