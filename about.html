About Us

MICHIKO Immigration is a Budapest-based immigration support service dedicated to helping individuals, families, and businesses navigate the often complex Hungarian immigration system. With a team of multilingual experts, we provide clear guidance and efficient solutions tailored to each client’s needs.

Our mission is to remove uncertainty and confusion from the immigration process, ensuring that every step is handled with accuracy, transparency, and care. From residence permits and work visas to family reunification and company filings, we make compliance simple and stress-free.

Our Mission

We are committed to maintaining integrity and building trust by delivering transparent, professional, and reliable immigration support for individuals and businesses in Hungary.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted partner in Hungary for immigration support services — known for efficiency, transparency, and unwavering commitment to client success.
