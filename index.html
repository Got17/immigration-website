About MICHIKO

MICHIKO Immigration is a Budapest-based support service that helps foreigners and companies manage Hungarian immigration and government procedures with clarity and confidence. Our top priority is understanding each client’s needs and mapping the best, most practical path through transparent communication.

We aim to be your trusted partner in Hungary—delivering high-quality, multilingual service and expert guidance. From residence and work permits to student paperwork and company filings, we remove uncertainty and keep every step secure, compliant, and on time.
